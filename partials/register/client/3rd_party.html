3rd Party Setup

We use Venmo to process client payments for tasks, milestones, and projects. Client payments will be requested by the freelancer through Venmo upon completion of a task, and invoices can be produced on-demand. Head to Venmo's website to create an account if you don't have one, and fill out your account details to allow us to interact with your account.
